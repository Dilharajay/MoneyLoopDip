Last Updated: June 1, 2025

1. Introduction

MoneyLoop ("we", "our", or "the platform") is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of all users accessing our digital ROSCA management service. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, disclose, and safeguard your information in accordance with the Information and Communication Technology Act No. 27 of 2003, Computer Crimes Act No. 24 of 2007, Electronic Transactions Act No. 19 of 2006, and Central Bank guidelines on digital financial services in Sri Lanka.

By using MoneyLoop, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of personal and financial data during registration and system use:

2.1 Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

  • Full Name
  • NIC Number
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Residential Address
  • Date of Birth
  • User Role (Admin or Member)

2.2 Financial & Transactional Data

  • Contribution amounts and timestamps
  • Transaction history and disbursement logs
  • Payment frequency and group configuration

2.3 Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Device and browser metadata
  • Login timestamps

3. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and process your data to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Authenticate login sessions
  • Facilitate contributions and disbursements
  • Send automated reminders and notifications
  • Generate analytics for admins and users
  • Improve system performance and user experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data to any third party. Data may be disclosed only in the following cases:

  • To internal administrators for service operation
  • To regulatory authorities upon lawful request (e.g., Central Bank of Sri Lanka)
  • To third-party service providers (e.g., cloud hosting or notification APIs), bound by confidentiality agreements
  • In cases of fraud detection, dispute resolution, or legal compliance

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security protocols to safeguard your information:

  • Passwords are hashed using strong encryption algorithms
  • Role-based access controls restrict unauthorized data access
  • Data backups are regularly maintained in secure environments

6. Data Retention

Your data will be retained only for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected. We may retain data for an extended period as required by regulatory bodies or for audit purposes. Upon request, your account and data may be permanently deleted unless restricted by legal obligation.

7. User Rights

In accordance with international privacy norms and local regulations:

  • You have the right to access, update, or correct your personal data
  • You can request account deactivation or data deletion
  • You may opt out of marketing emails and non-essential communications
  • Users can review privacy preferences via their account settings

8. Cookies and Tracking

MoneyLoop may use session cookies for authentication and maintaining user sessions. No third-party advertising or tracking cookies are used. You may control cookie behavior through your browser settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If a child's data is identified, it will be deleted immediately.

10. Policy Updates

This policy is subject to change based on legislative updates or system upgrades. Users will be notified via email or platform alerts of significant updates.
